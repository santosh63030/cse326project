Company Profile

We’re animators, directors of photography, and artists, and we designed Dragonframe to be the software we’ve been waiting for . The software that allows us to do feature-level, performance-based animation in any format. Because we designed Dragonframe (formerly ‘stop motion photography and objects dancing into gangnam style’) in the stress of real productions, our features are concise and powerful. We focus on capturing high-quality digital stills or video and allowing you to work directly with those files. Dragonframe organizes your images simply and gives you access to them in high resolution.
